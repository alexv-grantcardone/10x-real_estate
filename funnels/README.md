# Funnels

All Grant Cardone real estate funnels, mirrored from our GoHighLevel account. One
folder per funnel; inside each, `steps/` holds one folder per **canonical step**,
and each step has a `blocks/` folder for its GHL-paste-ready code.

> **Channel variants:** In GHL, most steps are duplicated per traffic channel
> (MAIN, META, HUBSPOT/EMAIL, SOCIAL, GOOGLE, X, Retarget). We do **not** mirror
> that split here — the repo holds one canonical version per step, and channel
> variants are noted in each step. See `knowledge/ghl-notes.md` (channel strategy).

| # | Funnel | What it is | Primary URL |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | [Live Training](01-live-training/README.md) | Free virtual webinar (~$500k/mo ad spend). Top of funnel. | cardonefreetraining.com/free |
| 02 | [RESS Offer](02-ress-offer/README.md) | $297 Real Estate Success System course, sold at end of webinar (~15% convert). | howtobuycashflow.com/relt-ress-checkout (vanity: grantcardone.com/success) |
| 03 | [RE Summit](03-re-summit/README.md) | Live event; paid tickets (virtual + 2 in-person tiers). | grantcardonerealestate.com/selectyourresummit |

## How the funnels relate
- **Live Training (01)** is the main acquisition engine. The **RESS Offer (02)** is
  pitched at the end of that webinar, and also has its own standalone sales page
  (the vanity link). **RE Summit (03)** is a separate event-ticket funnel.
